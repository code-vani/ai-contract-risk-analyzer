STATEMENT OF WORK — CLOUD INFRASTRUCTURE MIGRATION & API GATEWAY DEVELOPMENT

SOW Reference: SOW-2025-001
Effective Date: February 1, 2025
Master Services Agreement Date: January 1, 2025
Parties: TechM Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("Vendor") and Infosys Global Corp. ("Client")

================================================================================

Section 1 — Project Overview

1.1  This Statement of Work ("SOW") is issued pursuant to and governed by the Master
     Services Agreement ("MSA") dated January 1, 2025, between Vendor and Client.

1.2  The Vendor shall design, develop, test, and deploy a Cloud Infrastructure
     Migration solution and a RESTful API Gateway for Client's enterprise systems.
     The project encompasses migration of Client's on-premise workloads to AWS,
     development of a unified API Gateway, and delivery of all associated
     documentation and training materials.

1.3  All services under this SOW shall be performed in accordance with the terms
     of the MSA and the additional terms set forth herein. In the event of any
     conflict between this SOW and the MSA, the terms of this SOW shall govern
     with respect to the specific project described herein.

================================================================================

Section 2 — Payment Terms and Milestone Schedule

2.1  The total fixed fee for services under this SOW is INR 2,50,00,000 (Indian
     Rupees Two Crore Fifty Lakhs), payable in milestone-linked installments as
     set out in Section 2.3 below.

2.2  All milestone-based invoices submitted by the Vendor shall be paid by the
     Client within forty-five (45) days of receipt of the invoice and completion
     certificate for the applicable milestone. This timeline supersedes the general
     payment terms set forth in the MSA for the purposes of this SOW.

2.3  Milestone Payment Schedule:

     Milestone 1 — Project Kickoff and Requirements Sign-Off:     INR 25,00,000
     Milestone 2 — Architecture Design Document Approved:          INR 40,00,000
     Milestone 3 — Development Sprint 1 (Core API Gateway):       INR 50,00,000
     Milestone 4 — Development Sprint 2 (Migration Batch 1):      INR 50,00,000
     Milestone 5 — User Acceptance Testing Completion:            INR 50,00,000
     Milestone 6 — Production Go-Live and Hypercare:              INR 35,00,000

2.4  All payments shall be made by electronic funds transfer to the bank account
     designated in writing by Vendor. Vendor shall issue a tax-compliant GST invoice
     for each milestone payment.

================================================================================

Section 3 — Deliverables and Scope

3.1  All deliverables, technical specifications, acceptance criteria, and detailed
     project timelines for this SOW are as specified in Schedule 1 – Project Scope
     Document, which must be executed concurrently with this SOW and forms an
     integral part hereof. Client acknowledges that evaluation of deliverable
     completeness cannot proceed without the referenced Schedule 1.

3.2  The Vendor shall provide the following deliverables under this SOW:
     (a)  Current-state architecture assessment report;
     (b)  Target-state cloud architecture design document;
     (c)  API Gateway source code, unit tests, and deployment scripts;
     (d)  Data migration runbooks and rollback procedures;
     (e)  Production deployment package with release notes;
     (f)  Knowledge transfer documentation and training sessions.

3.3  All deliverables shall be submitted to Client's designated Project Manager for
     review and acceptance. Client shall provide written acceptance or a detailed
     list of deficiencies within ten (10) business days of submission. Failure to
     respond within this period shall constitute deemed acceptance.

================================================================================

Section 4 — Project Timeline

4.1  The project shall commence on the SOW Effective Date and shall be completed
     within nine (9) months, targeting a production go-live date of October 31, 2025,
     subject to Client's timely provision of access, approvals, and resources as
     specified in Schedule 1 – Project Scope Document.

4.2  Vendor shall provide Client with bi-weekly progress reports. Any risk to the
     timeline shall be communicated in writing within three (3) business days of
     Vendor becoming aware of such risk.

================================================================================

Section 5 — Late Delivery and Service Level Penalties

5.1  In the event that Vendor fails to deliver any milestone by the agreed date set
     forth in the milestone schedule, Client shall be entitled to levy service level
     deductions and penalty amounts as calculated and applied in accordance with the
     penalty formula and deduction table defined in SOW Section 9 — Penalty
     Calculations and Adjustments.

5.2  The maximum aggregate penalty that Client may levy against Vendor under this
     Section shall not exceed fifteen percent (15%) of the total SOW value.

5.3  Penalties shall be applied by way of deduction from the next milestone payment
     falling due after the penalty event, or by way of credit note if no further
     payments are due.

================================================================================

Section 6 — Intellectual Property and Code Ownership

6.1  Notwithstanding MSA Section 5 and notwithstanding any other provision of the
     MSA or this SOW, all software code, API schemas, database schemas, configuration
     scripts, architectural designs, and source artifacts developed by Vendor under
     this SOW shall remain the sole and exclusive intellectual property of the Vendor
     for a period of six (6) months following final delivery and Client acceptance.
     During this retention period, Vendor grants Client a limited, non-exclusive,
     non-transferable license to use the deliverables solely for internal business
     purposes.

6.2  Upon expiry of the six (6) month retention period described in Section 6.1,
     and upon receipt of all outstanding payments due under this SOW, Vendor shall
     execute an IP assignment agreement transferring all right, title, and interest
     in the Deliverables to Client.

================================================================================

Section 7 — Liability for Data Breaches and Cybersecurity Incidents

7.1  Notwithstanding MSA Section 7 and notwithstanding the general limitation of
     liability provisions set forth therein, the Vendor's liability for any data
     breach, cybersecurity incident, unauthorized access, data leakage, or
     compromise of Client data arising from Vendor's acts or omissions shall be
     uncapped and unlimited in amount.

7.2  In the event of a data breach attributable to Vendor, Vendor shall: (a) notify
     Client within 24 hours of discovery; (b) bear all costs of investigation,
     remediation, and notification to affected parties; (c) indemnify Client for all
     regulatory fines, penalties, third-party claims, and consequential losses arising
     from the breach, without limit.

================================================================================

Section 8 — Termination for Convenience

8.1  Client may terminate this Statement of Work for convenience at any time by
     giving seven (7) days written notice to the Vendor. Upon such termination, Client
     shall pay Vendor for all work completed and documented expenses incurred up to
     the effective date of termination.

8.2  Vendor may terminate this SOW for convenience by giving thirty (30) days written
     notice to Client, provided that no active milestone delivery is in progress.

================================================================================

Section 9 — Penalty Calculations and Adjustments

9.1  All penalty calculations, service level deduction percentages, and adjustments
     to the total contract value applicable under Section 5 of this SOW shall be
     determined by reference to the performance milestones, delivery dates, and
     measurement criteria defined in SOW Section 5 — Late Delivery and Service Level
     Penalties.

9.2  Penalty amounts are calculated as follows: for each week of delay beyond the
     agreed milestone date, a deduction of two percent (2%) of the applicable
     milestone value shall be applied, subject to the aggregate cap specified in
     Section 5.2.

9.3  All penalty deductions shall be documented in a penalty notice issued by Client
     and shall be subject to Vendor's right to dispute within five (5) business days
     of receipt.

================================================================================
