MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2025,
by and between TechM Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("Vendor") and Infosys Global Corp. ("Client").

================================================================================

Section 1 — Definitions

1.1  "Agreement" means this Master Services Agreement and all Statements of Work
     executed hereunder.

1.2  "Deliverables" means all work product, software, documentation, reports, and
     other tangible or intangible items developed by Vendor under any SOW.

1.3  "Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by one party to the
     other that is marked confidential or would reasonably be understood to be
     confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

1.4  "Statement of Work" or "SOW" means a written document executed by both parties
     that describes specific services, deliverables, timelines, and fees.

1.5  "Service Level Agreement" or "SLA" means the performance standards and metrics
     agreed upon in writing by both parties for any given engagement.

================================================================================

Section 2 — Scope of Services

2.1  Vendor shall provide the professional services described in each Statement of
     Work executed under this Agreement. Each SOW is incorporated herein by reference
     and made a part of this Agreement.

2.2  Vendor shall assign qualified personnel to perform the services described in each
     SOW. Vendor may, at its discretion, substitute personnel of equivalent skill and
     experience, subject to Client's prior written approval, which shall not be
     unreasonably withheld.

2.3  Client shall provide Vendor with timely access to systems, data, and personnel
     reasonably necessary for Vendor to perform the services. Delays caused by Client's
     failure to provide such access shall not constitute a breach by Vendor.

================================================================================

Section 3 — Term

3.1  This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and remain in full force and
     effect for a period of twelve (12) months, unless earlier terminated in accordance
     with Section 8 or extended by mutual written agreement of the parties.

3.2  Each Statement of Work shall specify its own performance period. Expiration of
     this Agreement shall not affect the validity or enforceability of any SOW whose
     performance period extends beyond the Agreement expiry, provided such SOW was
     executed prior to expiry.

================================================================================

Section 4 — Payment Terms

4.1  All invoices submitted by Vendor shall be paid by Client within thirty (30) days
     of the date of invoice receipt. Payment shall be made in Indian Rupees unless
     otherwise specified in the applicable SOW.

4.2  Any invoice amount not paid by the due date shall accrue interest at a rate of
     one and a half percent (1.5%) per month, or the maximum rate permitted by
     applicable law, whichever is lower, calculated from the due date until the date
     of actual payment.

4.3  Late payment fees and interest shall not be waived under any circumstances,
     including where Client disputes a portion of an invoice. Client shall pay the
     undisputed portion by the due date and the disputed portion within five (5)
     business days of dispute resolution.

4.4  Vendor shall submit invoices in accordance with the milestone schedule set forth
     in each SOW. Invoices must include a detailed description of services rendered,
     the applicable SOW reference number, and supporting documentation.

================================================================================

Section 5 — Intellectual Property

5.1  All Deliverables, work product, software code, designs, inventions, and other
     intellectual property created, developed, or produced by Vendor specifically for
     Client under any SOW shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Client upon
     full payment of all amounts due under the applicable SOW.

5.2  Vendor hereby assigns to Client all rights, title, and interest in and to such
     Deliverables, including all copyrights, patents, trade secrets, and other
     intellectual property rights embodied therein.

5.3  Vendor retains ownership of all pre-existing intellectual property, tools,
     frameworks, and methodologies ("Vendor Background IP") used in the performance
     of services. Vendor grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free
     license to use Vendor Background IP solely to the extent embedded in Deliverables.

================================================================================

Section 6 — Confidentiality

6.1  Each party agrees to hold the other party's Confidential Information in strict
     confidence and to use it solely for the purposes of performing its obligations
     under this Agreement.

6.2  Neither party shall disclose the other party's Confidential Information to any
     third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing party, except to
     employees or contractors who have a need to know and are bound by written
     confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those set forth herein.

6.3  The obligations of confidentiality set forth in this Section shall survive
     termination or expiration of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

================================================================================

Section 7 — Limitation of Liability

7.1  The total aggregate liability of either party to the other under or in connection
     with this Agreement and any SOW, whether in contract, tort (including negligence),
     breach of statutory duty, or otherwise, shall not exceed the total fees paid or
     payable by Client to Vendor under the applicable SOW during the twelve (12)
     months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

7.2  Neither party shall be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, special,
     punitive, or consequential damages, including but not limited to loss of revenue,
     loss of profits, loss of business opportunity, or loss of data, even if such
     party has been advised of the possibility of such damages.

7.3  The limitations set forth in this Section 7 shall not apply to: (a) death or
     personal injury caused by negligence; (b) fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation;
     or (c) any other liability that cannot be excluded or limited by applicable law.

================================================================================

Section 8 — Termination

8.1  Either party may terminate this Agreement or any individual Statement of Work for
     convenience by providing at least thirty (30) days prior written notice to the
     other party. Such notice shall specify the effective date of termination.

8.2  Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the
     other party commits a material breach and fails to cure such breach within fifteen
     (15) days after receiving written notice specifying the breach in reasonable detail.

8.3  Upon termination for any reason, Client shall pay Vendor for all services
     performed and expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination.
     Termination does not affect any outstanding SOW obligations that both parties
     have expressly agreed shall survive.

8.4  Sections 5, 6, 7, 9, and 10 shall survive expiration or termination of this
     Agreement for any reason.

================================================================================

Section 9 — Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

9.1  This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws
     of India, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

9.2  Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall first be
     referred to the senior management of both parties for resolution within thirty
     (30) days of written notice of the dispute.

9.3  If the dispute is not resolved within the period specified in Section 9.2, either
     party may refer the matter to binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of
     the Indian Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The seat of arbitration shall
     be Mumbai, India.

================================================================================

Section 10 — General Provisions

10.1  This Agreement, together with all Statements of Work executed hereunder,
      constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject
      matter and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and negotiations.

10.2  Any modification to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by
      authorized representatives of both parties.

10.3  If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the
      remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

10.4  Neither party may assign its rights or obligations under this Agreement without
      the prior written consent of the other party, which shall not be unreasonably
      withheld.

================================================================================
